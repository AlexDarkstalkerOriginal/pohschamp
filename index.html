FAQ

What is PogsChamp?
Pogs, generically called milk caps, is a game that was popular among children during the mid 1990s. We tried to transfer this game to the Nebulas blockchain.

Рow to start playing?
First you need to get a few caps to start playing. You can do this on the "Get pogs" tab. You can get 2 free packs per account.

How to buy from other players?
Visit market to buy crypto caps with certain parameters from other players.

How to attack?
Growing your lvl by attack opponent's pogs. The core parameter of every account is the lvl which defines chance to turn pog. You are able to gain exp by attacking others and winning. Game economy can’t be influenced by developers as there is no tools to change code after smart contracts are deployed and game is launched. For each victory you can get experience. The experience depends on the characteristics of the pog. Also, if you win, you have a chance to pick up the pog of the opponent. It depends on the property "drop" for each pog.

What influences my chance to turn pog?
The probability of reversing the pog depends on lvl, chane to turn(depends on lvl), the characteristics of the pog and the difference between the levels of the attacker and the defending one.

What is the maximum lvl?
20.

How often can I attack and be attacked?
You can attack every 2 minutes and be attacked every 5 minutes.
